    <h1>High-Precision Micro-Injection Pump R&D Project</h1>

    <p><strong>Project Overview:</strong> To eliminate volumetric flow errors caused by eccentric loads and backlash-induced stick-slip effects during micro-dose delivery, this project introduces a fully-constrained twin-rail precision injection system. The architecture successfully maintains the volumetric flow accuracy within an optimal regulatory margin of &plusmn;2% under continuous high-resistance scenarios.</p>


    <div class="highlight">

        <h3>💡 Core Mechanical Design Rationale (The 'Why')</h3>

        <ul>

            <li><strong>Fully-Constrained Twin-Rail Alignment:</strong> Deployed dual micro MGN9H linear guide rails to eliminate parasitic tilting moments and structural deflection under a 50 N peak injection load, ensuring consistent linear push-rod displacement.</li>

            <li><strong>Custom Integrated Anti-Backlash Gearbox:</strong> Utilized SolidWorks Top-Down modeling to design a split-gear 60T anti-backlash pinion with embedded torsion spring pockets, completely neutralizing rotational play during motor reversals.</li>

            <li><strong>Optimized Thermal Management:</strong> Selected a low-current, high-impedance NEMA 17 stepper motor to drastically minimize dynamic heat dissipation, preventing temperature-induced geometric tolerance shifts during long-term clinical infusion.</li>

        </ul>

    </div>
